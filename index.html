Who We Are

The Voices for Choice festival is proud to partner with the Planned Parenthood Action Fund to raise awareness and funds for reproductive rights. Our mission is to raise awareness and funds for reproductive rights. We aim to raise awareness and inspire change in our local communities. Voices for Choice is using our platform to educate, advocate, and promote the importance of equitable healthcare access for all. We fight for women's rights and for policies that will protect our reproductive rights to ensure that every woman has access to the healthcare she needs. Voices for Choice is here to create a brighter and equitable future for all.
