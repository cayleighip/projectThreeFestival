Voices for Choice Festival

March 7th - 9th, 2025

Join Us

Voices for Choice is hosting our first festival from March 7th to March 9th, 2025, aligning with the weekend of International Women’s Day. Celebrating women across the world and raising awareness about the issues of reproductive freedom in our country. Join us and support the cause. Food, discussions, lectures, workshops, activities and performances. A 1-Day festival ticket is $75 and the 3-Day festival tickets are $225. Tickets include wristbands to access free food at the food truck vendors.

Who We Are

The Voices for Choice festival is in partnership with the Planned Parenthood Action Fund to raise awareness and funds for the cause. We aim to raise awareness to local communities and inspire change in our local communities. The Voices for Choice is using our platform to promote education, advocate for reproductive rights, and emphasize the importance of social justice and healthcare access for all. We fight for women’s rights and aim to raise awareness and support policies that protect reproductive rights that ensures that every woman has access to the healthcare she needs. Voices for Choice will create a better future for all.
